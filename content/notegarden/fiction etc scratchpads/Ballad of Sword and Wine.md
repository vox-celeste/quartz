The thinking man's danmei. 

Every time I put it down for a few weeks and then come back to it the first chapter or two is a rough time because there are so many characters and job titles, but I enjoy that. There's a deep, borderline masochistic pleasure to reading a book that demands you pay attention and has the substance to justify it. Like exercise for the brain.
