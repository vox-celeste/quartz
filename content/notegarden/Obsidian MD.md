Two of the best things about Obsidian are

* its customizability and 
* its very enthusiastic community of users, but

these can also be cons… it can be overwhelming and lend itself to over-optimization, pursuit of a perfected workflow or the achievement of an “everything app” over using it for what it’s actually meant for, which is to write and organize my thoughts.

I currently have [[my vaults|three vaults]]… of which this is one.
