## folder structure

* **fiction etc scratchpads:** general notes on individual works—highly informal
* **lit notes (unprocessed):** transcriptions and blockquotes from nonfiction sources i want to reference later
* **arguments:** hypotheses, claims, observations. reasonably atomic.
* **questions:** gut feelings, curiosities, things to be researched later, putting pins in ideas to be thought-through
* **thematic:** grouping related concepts/arguments/questions, making connections

**DON’T OVERCOMPLICATE THINGS**
