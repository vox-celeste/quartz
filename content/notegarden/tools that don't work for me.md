Tools I’ve tried and abandoned at some point on my PKM journey, for one reason or another:
 
 * Joplin
 * writefreely/write.as
 * Fleeting Notes
 * Simplenote
 * OneNote
 * traditional bujo
 * Hobonichi paper planners (both Weeks and Techo) and Muji knockoffs
 * Notion
 * Trillium.next
 * Zettelkasten (sorta)

I don’t view these as failed experiments so much as learning opportunities that have illuminated my priorities; I’ve also taken bits and pieces from various approaches and carried them forward as I’ve developed my [[digital garden|digital garden ethos]].

I love trying new things and playing around with tech, but am not a person who actually knows how to use computers besides pasting commands into the CLI from a tutorial; this tension underpins much of my PKM adventures, lol.
